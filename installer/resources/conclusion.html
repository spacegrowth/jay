Jay is installed 🎉

Jay just opened and should be in your menu bar. One quick step to finish:

  1. When macOS asks, allow Jay under Accessibility (System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility). This enables the hotkey and screen-edge summon.
  2. Then summon it: press ⌘K, tap /, or push your pointer into the left screen edge.

Open Jay's Preferences from its menu-bar icon to change the shortcut, toggle screen-edge and login behavior, and add plugins.
