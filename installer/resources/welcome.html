Jay — summon everything, jump anywhere

Jay is a menu-bar launcher for every open tab, session, and window across your apps. This installer will place Jay in your Applications folder and open it.

  • Summon with ⌘K, tapping /, or by pushing your pointer into the left screen edge.
  • After install, macOS will ask you to allow Accessibility — this is what lets Jay watch for the hotkey and the screen-edge flick.
  • Jay runs from the menu bar (no Dock icon) and can start automatically at login.

Jay runs entirely on your Mac. No account, no telemetry, no cloud.
